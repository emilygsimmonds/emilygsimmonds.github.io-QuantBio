QuantBio: quantitative methods for biologists



Welcome to QuantBio


This website is your start point for information and learning resources related to Quantitative Methods in Biology.

In these pages you will find:

  • statistical theory
  • guides to presentation and analysis
  • worked examples
  • exercises
  • interactive apps
  • games

This material is designed to be a research tool and starting point for information. It is a resource to give you the skills you need to apply quantitative techniques to your biological work. These pages are a beginning and they do not contain all possible information on any topic. However, they should give you a background from which to explore further on your own.




Analysing data

Steps for data analysis:

  • Choose your research question
  • Look at the characteristics of your data
  • Choose an appropriate model
  • Estimate the parameters of the model
  • Quantify the uncertainty in the estimates
  • Check the model
  • Make conclusions

We will structure website sections in this way throughout.
